Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

TimePie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application TimePie (the "App").

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

The App allows you to create and track activities with custom names. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud backup features.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device identifiers, operating system version, and device type
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by third-party advertising services (see "Third-Party Services" below)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To display personalized advertisements
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and app usage to provide personalized ads. For more information about how Google uses data when you use our App, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings or by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app product.

Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for cloud backup functionality (if enabled). When you enable cloud backup, your app data is stored securely on Google's servers. For more information, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Google may collect payment information and transaction data. For more information, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your activity tracking data is stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted and stored on Google's secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (device identifiers, usage data)
  • Google Play Services: For cloud backup functionality (if enabled)
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases

These third parties are required to use your information only for the purposes we specify and in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the App
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
  • Remove ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app product
  • Disable cloud backup at any time through the App settings

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
